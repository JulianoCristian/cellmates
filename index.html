This is my 2013 Global Game Jam game, much more to come. Hit me up on Twitter with any feedback!

It's a two player game

To control the heart: mouse moves it up and down, left mouse button: increase heart rate, right button: decrease heart rate

Be careful with an elevated heart rate, heart attack could happen!

For the player: use a gamepad (experimental), or arrow keys to move, M to jump

If your gamepad isn't working right, you will need to unplug it to use the keyboard
